- Data Models:
A USER can have many HABITS.
HABITS can have many USERS.

A CATEGORY has many HABITS.
A HABIT belong to one CATEGORY.

AN ENTRY belongs to one HABIT.
A HABIT has many ENTRIES.

e.g.
Physical
- Running
- Yoga
- Weight Lifting
- Other

Mental
- Meditation
- Journaling
- Rest
- Other

Dietary
- Water
- Fruits & Veg
- Protein
- Other
